About Us

Flawless is a locally owned makeup and body waxing business founded by Giovanna Echevarria. With 11 years of experience in the cosmetics industry, Giovanna is passionate about helping others feel confident and beautiful. She started Flawless during the difficult times of the Covid-19 pandemic, driven by her desire to provide a safe and welcoming environment for her clients. Although this was a tough period to start a business, it forced her to adapt and learn the skills necessary to operate a successful business. Based in Boca Raton, Florida, Flawless offers a vast range of services in the areas of makeup and body waxing. Giovanna is dedicated to only using the highest quality products, ensuring that each client receives a personalized, professional experience. That's why she's created a warm and welcoming environment where you can relax, unwind, and leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated. At Flawless, you're more than just a client, you're a valued friend and part of the family. Whether you're looking for a special event makeup look or simply need a break from your daily routine, Flawless is here to help you feel your best. Book your appointment today!
