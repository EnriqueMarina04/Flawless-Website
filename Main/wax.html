Our Wax

Our wax has three primary ingredients. First, it has the highest quality pine resin, which is found in Germany. Pine resin provides the benefits of being antimicrobial and increasing circulation. The conifers from this pine resin are recognized as one of the best in the world, and that results in the cooling temperature upon application, even though the wax is hot. This wax is also not synthetic, which is one of the major issues of other products. The second ingredient is the high-quality beeswax, which creates a very good grip onto the hairs. In turn, this results in less applications being needed, thus causing less irritation to the skin. The final ingredient is paraffin. This is used to boost moisture levels and soften the skin.
